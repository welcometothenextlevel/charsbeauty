Permanent Hair Reduction

SHR Hair Removal

Virtually pain-free. Permanently effective. Safe for all skin types.

SHR (Super Hair Removal) uses rapid, low-energy pulses — up to 10 shots per second — combined with In-Motion gliding technology to gently and gradually heat the hair follicle, delivering safe, comfortable and long-lasting hair reduction. Unlike traditional IPL, SHR works effectively on all skin types, including sensitive and darker skin tones. Most clients notice early signs of reduced regrowth within just one or two sessions, and the sensation is pretty much pain-free — just a warm, gentle heat, nothing like the sharp snap of older technologies.

Because SHR builds heat gradually rather than firing a single intense pulse, it's especially well-suited to areas that are usually more sensitive, like the face, underarms and bikini line. With consistent sessions (typically 6–8 sessions for visible results, up to 10 for a full course), most clients see a noticeable reduction in hair growth fairly quickly, with continued improvements in texture and speed of regrowth over the course of treatments.

Most clients describe it as warm and comfortable — often compared to a hot stone massage. There are no high-intensity bursts like traditional IPL.

SHR targets melanin (pigment) in the hair follicle, so results depend on how much melanin is present in your hair. Darker hair (black, brown, dark blonde) typically responds better and faster since there's more melanin for the light to target. Lighter hair — such as blonde, red, or very fine hair — can still respond well, but usually needs more sessions to achieve the same results, since there's less melanin present.

Laser uses a single concentrated wavelength in strong pulses — effective, but can be more painful, with a higher risk of pigmentation changes on darker skin. SHR glides continuously over the area using lower heat, building up energy gradually rather than one intense zap.
