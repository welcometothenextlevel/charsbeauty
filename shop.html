Professional Korean Skincare

Healthy Skin Starts at Home

Did you know that around 80% of your skin results come from your daily homecare routine, while professional treatments make up the remaining 20%?

Professional treatments accelerate your results, treat concerns more effectively and work at a deeper level — but what you do every day at home is what maintains those results and keeps your skin healthy between appointments.

That's why professional skincare goes hand in hand with your in-salon treatments.

At Char's Beauty Room, I carefully select professional Korean skincare that delivers visible results while supporting your skin barrier and long-term skin health. Every recommendation is personalised because no two skins are the same.

Why Korean Professional Skincare?

Korean skincare is recognised worldwide for combining innovative technology with high-quality ingredients that focus on long-term skin health rather than quick fixes.

  • ✓ Supports and repairs the skin barrier
  • ✓ Advanced ingredients backed by science
  • ✓ Gentle yet highly effective formulations
  • ✓ Targets concerns without compromising skin health
  • ✓ Suitable for a wide range of skin types, including sensitive skin
